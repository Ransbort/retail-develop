Retail Suite is a modern POS and retail management system built on ERPNext and Vue.js.

It provides a fast and intuitive point-of-sale experience for small and medium retail businesses with full integration to ERPNext backend services.

Key Features:

- Modern POS interface with dark and light mode support
- Barcode scanning and product search capabilities
- Advanced serial and batch number search with direct selling from tracked items.
- Advanced cart management with sale, return, and draft modes
- Discount and tax handling for invoices
- Offline-capable POS operations
- Cash flow tracking and reconciliation tools
- Shift dashboard with sales and payment analytics, including detection of reconciliation issues.
- Barcode generation and management tools
- Multi-language support including (Arabic and English)
- Payment reconciliation screen to match and settle payments against invoices.
- Mobile barcode scanning powered by Quagga that automatically adds products to the cart in real time.
- Shift closing with full MOP (Method of Payment) reconciliation to ensure payment accuracy across all methods before closing.

Built on ERPNext, leveraging inventory, accounting and stock management systems.

Retail Suite is designed to improve retail operations efficiency, speed up checkout processes, and provide real-time insights for store management.
