# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** June 16, 2026

## 1. Overview

Retail Suite is an open-source application built on the Frappe Framework. This Privacy Policy explains how data is handled when you use the Software.

## 2. Data We Do Not Collect

Retail Suite does **not** collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. All data entered into the Software (customers, invoices, products, etc.) remains entirely within your own self-hosted Frappe/ERPNext instance.

## 3. Data You Control

All data is stored locally on your own server. You are solely responsible for:

- Securing your server and database.
- Managing access permissions for your users.
- Backing up your data.
- Complying with applicable data protection laws in your region.

## 4. Third-Party Services

Retail Suite does not integrate with any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services. However, it runs on top of:

- [Frappe Framework](https://frappeframework.com/)
- [ERPNext](https://erpnext.com/)

Please refer to their respective privacy policies for information about their data handling practices.

## 5. Cookies

Retail Suite itself does not set cookies. Any cookies present are set by the underlying Frappe Framework for session management purposes.

## 6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Effective Date" above.

## 7. Contact

For any privacy-related questions, please open an issue on [GitHub](https://github.com/AhmedAbokhatwa/retail-suite/issues).
